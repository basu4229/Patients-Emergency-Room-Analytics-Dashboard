Patient Emergency Room Analytics Dashboard
Project Overview:
The Patient Emergency Room Analytics Dashboard is an end-to-end Power BI healthcare analytics project designed to analyze emergency room performance and patient service efficiency. The dashboard transforms raw healthcare data into actionable insights that help hospital administrators monitor operational performance, patient flow, and service quality.
The solution focuses on identifying patterns in patient wait times, satisfaction levels, visit trends, demographic behavior, and referral statistics using interactive Power BI visualizations.

Dashboard Preview
The dashboard provides a complete overview of emergency room activity through KPI cards, trend analysis, demographic heat maps, and dynamic charts.
Key business questions addressed:

How long do patients wait on average?
What is the overall patient satisfaction level?
Which periods experience the highest and lowest patient traffic?
What patient groups require more healthcare attention?
How do referral and walk-in patterns compare?
What percentage of appointments are administrative vs non-administrative?
